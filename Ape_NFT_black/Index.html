Getting Started

SETUP

Setup a MetaMask Wallet.

Looking to kick off your NFT collection, but not sure where to begin? The first thing you'll need is a crypto wallet, which will store your method of payment and allow you to access your NFT.

FUND ETH

Fund MetaMask with ETH.

Ether is the native currency of the Ethereum network and it's commonly abbreviated to ETH, which is its most common signifier. You need ETH to pay for some of your interactions with the blockchain, and to pay for the items you buy.
